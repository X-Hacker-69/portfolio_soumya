Government Healthcare Scheme Awareness & Utilisation

Survey-based research project assessing public knowledge and actual uptake of central and state government health schemes.

Surveyed community members across demographic groups on awareness of Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY) coverage
Mapped awareness gaps against Odisha-specific schemes (BSKY, etc.) and district-level healthcare infrastructure
Identified key barriers: documentation requirements, facility distance, and lack of intermediary guidance
Presented findings with actionable recommendations for improving community health outreach strategies
